Unique Methodology

Conventional educational methods often leave you
having to remember the concepts you were taught or
trying to figure out how to apply them. The London
School of Photography method leaves you applying what
you learned naturally and without effort.


Similar to anything you have already mastered, in our
photography courses and workshops you learn by direct
personal discovery. From this experience, a new skill is
yours forever.
